Tourism In Saudi Arabia

Analyzing tourism trends in Saudi Arabia to understand domestic and international tourism patterns Purpose: Provide insights into the behavior of domestic and international tourists, types of accommodation, and travel purposes. Business Problem: The need to improve tourism strategies to support Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030, attract more tourists, and increase tourism spending. Objectives: Analyze tourist numbers, tourism spending, geographical distribution, and tourism trends over time, aiming to guide infrastructure development and identify opportunities to promote domestic and international tourism. Objectives: 1-Studying the changes in tourism indicators across different years 2-Identifying the most popular tourism activities among visitors, such as entertainment, religious purposes, or for investment and business. 3- Analysis of domestic tourism data versus inbound tourism to determine which contributes more to the tourism sector. 4- Identify the destinations that attracted the most tourists and to determine the common factors among them. .

Solar Energy Consumption

conducted a feasibility analysis of solar energy projects across U.S. states, evaluating over 50 solar projects and assessing key metrics such as project growth rates, financial viability, and market competitiveness. Active Outfitters

Conducted a comprehensive quality of life analysis, benchmarking Saudi Arabia against high-ranking countries using key KPIs such as life expectancy, homeownership rate, and unemployment rate. To support this analysis, I implemented Extract, Transform, and Load (ETL) workflows to clean, format, and merge datasets from multiple reliable sources, including the World Bank, Legatum Prosperity Index, and the Sustainable Development Report. Additionally, I researched best practices from benchmark countries and developed interactive Power BI dashboards to uncover meaningful correlations. Based on these insights, I provided recommendations to accelerate the progress of Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030 Quality of Life Program.

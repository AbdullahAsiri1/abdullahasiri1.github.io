About Me

Hi, I'm Abdullah Asiri.
Thanks for visiting my portfolio!
.

My name is Abdullah Al-Asiri, and I graduated from King Khalid University in Abha with a degree in Computer Science. I have always been interested in the field of Artificial Intelligence (AI), and I studied Data Mining and Machine Learning. After graduation, I joined the Saudi Digital Academy (SDA) in the Business Intelligence specialization for a 12-week program (480 hours), where I learned various data analysis techniques such as Excel, Python, Power BI, Tableau, and SQL. During this time, I worked on several projects related to data analysis and business intelligence, and I gained a solid understanding of financial concepts to help stakeholders make well-informed decisions with high-quality data.

I am passionate about applying AI techniques to optimize business processes and support data-driven decision-making with advanced solutions. I aspire to be at the forefront of the digital transformation and look forward to engaging in more projects and research opportunities to enhance my skills in AI and data analysis.
